Robots Battle Arena

C | Python3 | Yacc & Lex

  • Text parser
  • Virtual Machine
  • Turns and Actions controller
  • Data piped into Pygame
  • Visual Interface

Semester long project, in which me and my group made a battle arena simulator from scratch. Players have a description of the map and several possible intructions. With five individual robots they must collect explosive cristals and place them on the enemy base, bofore that is done to their base.

Attack in groups, focus on defense, sneak in by the edges or go straight ahead. You make your strategy. You have to take care of your robots internal memory as that is a limited resource and be proactive as your turn end fast.

Code like you would do in Python. Your robot have memory, so you can set your variables. Memorizations also allows them to understand the functions you define. They see all things as targets or obstacles so they do not have classes. Yet.
